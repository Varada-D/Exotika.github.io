About Exotika


Exotica is a marketplace for resorts across India, which provides a one-place integration for interactions between various stakeholders. This includes:-

  • > Visitor (Tourist) - Resort Owner
  • > Visitor (Tourist) - Local Handicraft Dealer
  • > Resort Owner - Product/Service Provider

With an aim of providing a seamless interaction of stakeholders, we at Exotika provide distinct portals for visitors, resort owners, product/service providers and local handicraft dealers. By the virtue of effortless proximity, Exotika ensures credibility, transparency and responsibility in all interactions and transactions.





About the Portals


Visitor/Tourist Portal

Through the visitor portal, a user can view the resorts, local fairs/festival/event calendar and the art and craft specific to the place selected by him/her. The User would have the facility for booking a room/package of the listed resorts on signing in. He/She will also be able to view booking/transaction history through his/her dashboard.

Go to Visitor Portal

Resort Owner Portal

Through the resort owners portal, a user (on signing in), can analyse the booking history/trends of their resort and list out the available rooms, packages and offers, and allow online booking. Apart from that, they can also post about product/service requirements, interact with product/service providers and view contract status.

Go to Resort Owner Portal

Vendor Portal

Through the vendor portal, a user can view the requirements of products/services as listed out by the resort owner, filtering them by city and type. On registering, the user is also notified about these requirements through mail and SMS. The user can to view contract status on signing-in. The requirements could be of groceries, cutleries, stationaries, toiletaries, plumbers, electricians, rentals etc.

Go to Vendor Portal

Local Handicraft Dealer Portal

Through the local handicraft dealers portal, a user can list out the region-specific handicrafts they provide, along with their business details, including address and contact information.

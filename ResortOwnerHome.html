Why choose Exotika for Resorts?

Exotika is the one-stop destination for resort ownes for all their resort needs. Need to increase tourist outreach? Need stationery, electronics, cutlery, electronic items, or any other goods and/or services? Worry not, we got you covered!

So what all functionalities do you, as a resort owner, get on registering with us?

Tourist Outreach

You can list out the available rooms, packages and offers, of your resort, and allow online booking. This would allow you to expand your reach to a wider audience with your online presence on our marketplace. What's more? You can also analyze the booking history/trends of your resort, and plan packages and offers accordingly!

Resort Needs

We are aware of the fact that resorts are constantly in the need of some or the other kind of supplies (products and services), right from groceries to event management/planning to rentals to electricians/plumbers and so much more!

To help you with all your needs, we have collaborated with product/service vendors throughout the country so that your demands/requirements can be fulfilled on-site itself! You can easily interact with product/service providers and view contract status.

You can post your requirements through your dashboard on logging in, and we'll send the notification of the requirements to vendors in your city. They can then give you a proposal. It's now in your hands to get the best bait and select the vendor that appeals to you the most.
